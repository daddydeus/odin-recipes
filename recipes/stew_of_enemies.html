Stew of Enemies

What is Stew of Enemies?

If you are in the need for a satisfying meal to emjoy after a long day at war with the AI invaders, then this one pot easy beef stew is perfect! Packed full of veggies and loaded with the flavorful flesh of your slain enemies, this quick to prep meal is perfect for the returning warrior.


Lets go through the list of ingredients needed for making this dish


Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat from slain enemies cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 3 tablespoons all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 onion roughly chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • ¼ cup tomato paste
  • 1 pound potatoes peeled and cubed
  • 6 carrots roughly chopped
  • 3 celery sticks sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cups beef broth from preparing the tender flesh of your slain enemies
  • Fresh parsley for serving

Now that we have our ingredients, time to get cooking!


Steps

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper in a large bowl. Sprinkle the flour on top and toss well to combine.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large Dutch oven or pot over medium heat. Brown the beef on all sides, stirring occasionally, about 5-7 minutes
  3. Add the onions and garlic, and saute until lightly browned and fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato paste and continue to cook with the onions and garlic, about 1-2 more minutes.
  5. Add the potatoes, carrots, celery, thyme and basil along with the broth and stir to combine, scraping any brown bits in the pot.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 90 minutes, or until the beef is very tender.
  7. Remove the bay leaves, season with fresh parsley and serve warm.

Enjoy your meal!
